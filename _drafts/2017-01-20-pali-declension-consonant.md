#### 10. 以 -an 收尾的阳性词：<span class="box">rājan</span>

||单数|复数|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|第一格|rāj**ā**|rāj**ā**, rāj**āno**|
|第二格|rāj**aṃ**, rāj**ānaṃ**|rāj**ā**, rāj**āno**|
|第三格|rañ**ñā**, rāj**inā**, rāj**ena**|rāj**ehi**, rāj**ŭhi**|
|第五格|rañ**ñā**|rāj**ehi**, rāj**ŭhi**|
|第四、六格|rañ**ño**, rāj**ino**, rāj**assa**|rañ**ñaṃ**, rāj**ānaṃ**, rāj**ūnaṃ**|
|第七格|rañ**ñe**, rañ**ñi**, rāj**ini**|rāj**esu**, rāj**ŭsu**|
|呼格|rāj**ă**|-|

#### 11. 以 -an 收尾的中性词：<span class="box">kamman</span>

||单数|复数|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|第一、二格|kamm**a**, kamm**aṃ**|kamm**āni**, kamm**ā**|
|第三格|kamm**anā**, kamm**unā**, kamm**ena**|kamm**ehi**|
|第五格|kamm**unā**|kamm**ehi**|
|第四、六格|kamm**uno**, kamm**assa**|kamm**ānaṃ**|
|第七格|kamm**ani**|kamm**esu**|

- 单数第二格也可以是 kamm**ānaṃ**。

#### 12. 以 -vat, -mat 收尾的阳性词：<span class="box">bhagavat</span>

||单数|复数|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|第一格|bhagav**ā**, bhagav**anto**|bhagav**anto**, bhagav**antā**|
|第二格|bhagav**aṃ**, bhagav**antaṃ**|bhagav**anto**, bhagav**ante**|
|第三格|bhagav**atā**, bhagav**antena**|bhagav**antehi**|
|第五格|bhagav**atā**|bhagav**antehi**|
|第四、六格|bhagav**ato**, bhagav**antassa**|bhagav**ataṃ**, bhagav**antānaṃ**|
|第七格|bhagav**ati**, bhagav**ante**|bhagav**antesu**|
|呼格|bhagav**ă**, bhagav**anta**, bhagav**aṃ**|-|

特例：<span class="box">bhavat</span>

||单数|复数|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|第一格|bhav**aṃ**|bhav**anto**, bho**nto**, bhav**antā**|
|第二格|bhav**antaṃ**, bho**taṃ**|bhav**ante**, bho**nte**|
|第三、五格|bhav**atā**, bho**tā**|bhav**antehi**|
|第四、六格|bhav**ato**, bho**to**, bhav**antassa**|bhav**ataṃ**, bhav**antānaṃ**|
|第七格|bhav**ati**, bhav**ate**|bhav**antesu**|
|呼格|bho, bhan**ta**, bhan**te**, bhav**ante**|bhan**te**, bhav**anto**, bho**nto**|

#### 13. 以 -at 收尾的现在分词：<span class="box">gacchat</span>

||单数|复数|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|第一格|gacch**aṃ**, gacch**anto**|gacch**anto**, gacch**antā**|
|第二格|gacch**antaṃ**|gacch**ante**|

- 表中未列部分同以 **-vat, -mat** 收尾的阳性词。
